About

She is very passionate about issues that affect the youth and makes an effort to think of solutions that will make a difference and inspire youth to not only dream big but be big! She’s a fast learner and takes direction very well. She is ambitious, reliable, responsible, patient, hardworking, organised and good with time. She’s charismatic and loves to talk and write about anything and everything. She’s a people’s person and loves helping where she can.

Background

Growing up in a radio family, Boipelo tried to shy away from radio to venture in her own career but later knew that the creative space is where her purpose lies. Knowing that she had a passion for content production and writing she decided to take it seriously and enrolled at Boston Media House to study Media. She completed her studies in Media Practice, while presenting her radio show at her school’s station. After graduating she took on the role of Production Manager at 178 Media and resigned to grow her skills and venture into writing. This was when she started her career as a copywriter at Umuzi where she is currently.
